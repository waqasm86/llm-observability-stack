# EdgeLLM Observability Business Model

## Open-Source Community

The public project provides:

- Helm chart and k3s deployment profiles
- NVIDIA edge GPU setup helpers
- Prometheus/Grafana dashboards and alert rules
- repeatable benchmark script and sanitized evidence format

The open-source layer supports adoption, reproducibility, contributor feedback, and qualified pilot
discussions.

## Enterprise Pilot

A paid two-to-six-week edge LLM observability pilot can include:

- workload and device baseline
- reproducible benchmark report
- dashboard and retention setup
- alert and runbook setup
- security and data-flow review
- production recommendation and rollout risks

Commercial terms should be scoped by device count, workloads, integration requirements, support
hours, and evidence/reporting needs.

## OEM/SI Bundle

A future OEM or systems-integrator offer can include:

- validated image/profile for NVIDIA laptops and workstations
- benchmark matrix across approved hardware
- deployment, support, and debugging guide
- co-marketing or reference architecture after formal partner approval

## Target Buyers

- enterprise AI platform teams
- IT departments
- field engineering organizations
- universities and laboratories
- OEM and SI partners

## Positioning Boundaries

Avoid:

- consumer-first positioning
- unlicensed bundled model claims
- claims of Lenovo or NVIDIA certification
- production-readiness claims without workload, security, and customer evidence

The near-term commercial objective is a design-partner pilot, not a broad unsupported hardware
product claim.
